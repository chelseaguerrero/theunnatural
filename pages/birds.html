About

Didn't have a lot of friends, huh?

Do you ever wondered what it’s like to be a bird? There was probably a time when you just wished you were a bird. You would have the ability to travel anywhere or just go for a soar to cool off on a hot day. If birds could talk, we would all probably ask them what it’s like. Are they afraid of heights? Can they kindly not poop on our heads? If birds could talk, we would have another species, other than humans, to converse with. Also, we could finally have the bird messengers we found so cool in stories.
